# BillCraft Product Overview

BillCraft is an AI-powered invoice management system designed for small businesses and freelancers. The platform combines professional invoicing capabilities with intelligent AI assistance to streamline business operations.

## Core Features

- **AI-Powered Assistant**: Context-aware chat AI that helps with invoice creation, form filling, and business guidance
- **Professional Invoicing**: 10+ customizable invoice templates with multi-currency support
- **Client Management**: Dedicated client database with full contact management
- **Trial System**: 3-month free trial with full feature access (no credit card required)
- **Real-time Dashboard**: Statistics, revenue tracking, and analytics
- **Status Tracking**: Draft, Sent, Paid, Overdue, and Cancelled invoice states

## User Experience

- Clean, modern interface with smooth animations (Framer Motion)
- Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Global floating AI assistant available on all pages
- Professional templates for different industries
- Auto-generated sequential invoice numbers per user

## Business Model

- Free trial: 3 months full access
- Paid tiers: Professional and Enterprise plans
- Trial tracking with usage statistics and conversion metrics
